WHAT IS THIS

uSlugg is a 10,000-piece animated, fully on-chain generative art collection. Each slugg is 1–3 panels of patterns (noise · checkerboard · stripes · dashes · dots · diagonal · random-blocks) that smear across a Canvas2D loop, fade, and re-emerge forever.

FULLY ON-CHAIN. The seed lives in contract storage. The renderer and the ~10KB Canvas2D JS runtime are bytecode-stored. tokenURI returns an animation_url data URI — your wallet renders the animation live, no IPFS, no off-chain hosting.

404 HYBRID + V4 HOOK. USLUG is an ERC-20 (3 decimals) joined to an ERC-721. Holding 1.000 USLUG = owning 1 Slugg NFT. On mainnet a v4 afterSwap hook re-rolls the on-chain seed every swap, so swap-buying USLUG mints fresh sluggs and selling burns them. Selling and re-buying gives the next holder a brand-new slugg.

CLAIM & UNCLAIM. claim() lifts a slugg out of the 404 into a standalone ERC-721 with EIP-2981 royalties so it lists cleanly on OpenSea / Blur. unclaim() wraps it back. Small ETH fees on each side (testnet uses cheap fees).

↓ This testnet uses a faucet instead of a Uniswap pool — request 5 USLUG (= 5 sluggs) per hour. Mainnet uses real swap-driven mints.
