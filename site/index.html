WHAT IS THIS
uSlugg is animated generative art. Each piece is 1-3 panels of patterns (noise / checkerboard / stripes / etc.) that smear across the canvas, fade, and re-emerge forever.
Fully on-chain: seed in contract storage · renderer + JS runtime are bytecode-stored · your wallet animates them live.
Testnet uses a public-mint contract. Real launch will be a v4 hook + 404 hybrid — sluggs mint as you swap.
